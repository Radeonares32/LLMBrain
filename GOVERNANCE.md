# Governance

LLM Brain is an open-source project that relies on the contributions of its community. This document outlines the governance model for the project.

## Roles

### Users
Users are community members who have a need for the project. They are the most important members of the community and without them the project would have no purpose.

### Contributors
Contributors are community members who contribute in concrete ways to the project. Anyone can become a contributor, and contributions can take many forms.

### Maintainers
Maintainers are contributors who have shown that they are committed to the long term success of a project. Maintainers have write access to the repository and can merge pull requests.

## Decision Making

Decisions about the future of the project are made through discussion with all members of the community. In general, we prefer to reach consensus. When consensus cannot be reached, the core maintainers have the final say.

## Adding Maintainers

New maintainers are added by existing maintainers. A contributor may be nominated to become a maintainer if they have shown a sustained commitment to the project and have a track record of high-quality contributions.
